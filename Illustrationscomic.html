Project: Illustrations.02

ILLUSTRATION
COMICBOOK.

Concept

This project involves the comprehensive translation of a narrative into a visual medium, beginning with a structured script breakdown that identifies key plot points, central conflicts, and resolutions. During the narrative phase, you will define character aesthetics and determine the panel count necessary for a comic-style delivery, culminating in a detailed scene-by-scene storyboard. The second phase shifts to visual development, where you will iterate through thumbnails, rough sketches, and polished drafts to produce final artwork. Throughout this process, the objective is to maintain character consistency and a cohesive tone while mastering the pacing and transitions essential for effective visual storytelling.

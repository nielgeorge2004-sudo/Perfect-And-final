About Me

Hi, I’m Niel George. I’m a communication design student who lives in the space where classic aesthetics meet modern digital energy. My days are usually spent in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, turning messy, scattered ideas into clean visual stories. I love a good creative challenge—whether that’s diving into the historical geometry of the Modhera Sun Temple or building out high-energy campaigns for brands like boAt. For me, it’s all about finding that sweet spot between cultural depth and commercial punch. While I’m still finishing my studies and refining my craft, I’m fully geared up for what’s next. When I’m not pixel-pushing, I’m probably hunting for new music or getting way too distracted by the natural design of a star fruit. Just here to keep learning, keep designing, and see where the next project leads.

Project: Creative Image Making.01

LUXURY
PERFUME.

Concept

This project involves designing a six-piece ultra-luxury perfume collection inspired by the star fruit (carambola). You will translate its distinctive five-point radial symmetry, crisp angular ridges, and translucent waxy gradients into a sophisticated visual language for high-end bottle design. The goal is to harmonize the fruit’s exotic, sculptural essence with the material elegance and ergonomic standards required for the premium fragrance market.

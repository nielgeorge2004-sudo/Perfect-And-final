HISTORY
JOURNAL.

The Brief

Drawing from a field study of the Sun Temple at Modhera and Rani ki Vav, this project tasks you with translating the solar symbolism and architectural grandeur of these heritage sites into a creative visual narrative. You will produce a stylized Journal Report that functions as an expressive exploration of Indian art and design history, blending sketches, technical notes, and personal observations. By integrating diverse visual elements—such as doodles, color coding, and timelines—the goal is to develop a unique graphic language that captures the structural and cultural essence of these landmarks in a highly personalized, editorial format.
